App:

MediCare - Manage your appointments better.

Description:

The app allows you to manage your appointments either as a Doctor or as a Patient.

As a patient, you can request an appointment, view your upcoming appointments and view your medical reports and surgeries statuses. As a doctor, you can view your patients and the appointment reason as well ass appointment requests and medical reports and surgeries.
